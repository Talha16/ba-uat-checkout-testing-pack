# UAT Overview & Scope

## Purpose
The purpose of UAT (User Acceptance Testing) is to validate that the checkout flow meets business expectations and is ready for release.

## In Scope
- Proceed to checkout
- Guest checkout
- Shipping address validation
- Shipping method selection
- Payment validation and authorization handling (conceptual)
- Order review and confirmation display

## Out of Scope
- Performance/load testing
- Security penetration testing
- Payment gateway certification testing
- Post-purchase flows (returns/refunds)

## UAT Participants (Conceptual)
- Business Analyst (UAT coordination, documentation, triage)
- Business stakeholder / Product owner (final validation)
- QA representative (support testing)
- Developer support (fixes and clarifications)

## Entry Criteria
- User stories and acceptance criteria approved
- Test environment available
- Test data available (sample orders, addresses, payment test cards)

## Exit Criteria
- All critical defects resolved or accepted with mitigation
- UAT test cases executed with documented outcomes
- Stakeholder sign-off recorded
